About Us

About Us

Cadence in the Clouds is the live music extension of Spice on the Beat, a Philadelphia-based Hip-Hop blog. It is a developmental concert series that takes place three times a year and doesn't prioritize highlighting artists with the highest streaming numbers. Instead, it showcases artists who deserve more opportunities than they are currently receiving.

The events occur in different locations on Mondays—popular industry nights in the city—while partnering with local Philadelphia-based businesses. This demonstrates to artists that they don't need to move to New York or Los Angeles to have a thriving career; they can build one here with the right resources.

Cadence in the Clouds strives to bring those resources into one room, celebrating every individual who puts their passion at the forefront of their career and dares to take risks rather than follow the paths of conventionality.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

While Cadence in the Clouds is predominantly a music concert, it is so much more than that. It creates a room that celebrates art, demonstrating the concept in all its varieties, from the visual to the culinary arts. It's also a show not necessarily just for music lovers but for the creatives who contribute to the artistic scenes we all enjoy, giving them a night out and an experience with resources, connections, and opportunities.

Mission

01

To facilitate and encourage collaboration among Philadelphia artists and businesses to stimulate the local economy, create new opportunities, and showcase an artistic community that is often overlooked.

02

To create a concert with benefits for the artists, with tickets including resources such as artist biographies, music tech equipment, scheduled calls with marketing agencies, and other takeaways that are unconventional yet useful to one's career.

03

To highlight Hip-Hop, specifically, in an elevated manner in a city rich with roots in the culture. Philadelphia often gets forgotten in the origin story of Hip-Hop, and it's getting more lost as society shuns it. But it's impactful, and it's time to showcase it in a light that demonstrates its artistry and importance.

The Event

Cadence occurs 2-3 times a year, with the first event of 2025 on Monday, May 12th at Level 28. We rotate venues and artists for each event, showcasing new talent and unconventional locations around the city. The night will feature a wide variety of artists, with 2 Hip-Hop performances, a DJ, complimentary bites from a local chef, complimentary bites from a local pastry chef, an art exhibit featuring a Philly-based artist, a live visual artist, a bar menu created by 1 of Philadelphia's top bartenders highlighting the artists, and so much more.

We would additionally love to create swag bags for each person to go home with. We want these bags to include things that would tantalize the artistic mind, whether vinyl, resources, coupons, technology, or anything in between. If you cannot contribute financially but would be willing to explore this option, we would love to discuss it further!
